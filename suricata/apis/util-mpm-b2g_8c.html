Detailed Description

Author
Victor Julien victo.nosp@m.r@in.nosp@m.linia.nosp@m.c.ne.nosp@m.t

Implementation of the SBNDMq pattern matching algorithm.

Ideas:

  • B2g does a full match in the search of up to 'm' characters, in case of a case insensitive search we could say it's match if the pattern is of len 'm' or just compare the rest of the chars.
