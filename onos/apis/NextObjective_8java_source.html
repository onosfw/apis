25 /**
26  * Represents a nexthop which will be translated by a driver
27  * into the appropriate group or actions needed to implement
28  * the egress function.
29  *
30  * A next objective is made up of a collection of traffic treatments
31  * associated with a type. These types are:
32  *
33  * - Hashed
34  * - Broadcast
35  * - Failover
36  * - Simple
37  *
38  * These types will indicate to the driver what the intended behavior is.
39  * For example, a broadcast next objective with a collection of output
40  * treatments will indicate to a driver that all output actions are expected
41  * to be executed simultaneously. The driver is then free to implement this
42  * as a group or a simple action list.
43  */
44 @Beta
45 public interface NextObjective extends Objective {
