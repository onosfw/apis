Detailed Description

Denotes the highlighting to be applied to a link.

Flavor is a closed set of NO-, PRIMARY-, or SECONDARY- highlighting. Mod is an open ended set of additional modifications (CSS classes) that may also be applied. Note that MOD_OPTICAL and MOD_ANIMATED are pre-defined mods. Label text may be set, which will also be displayed on the link.
