String org.onosproject.flowanalyzer.FlowAnalyzer.analyze ( )

Analyzes and prints out a report on the status of every flow entry inside the network.

The possible states are: Cleared (implying that the entry leads to a host), Cycle (implying that it is part of cycle), and Black Hole (implying that the entry does not lead to a single host).

result string
